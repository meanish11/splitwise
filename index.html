Quick Start

💡 How to use TripWise

Get your group set up in under a minute — no account, no downloads.

1
🏕️
✨ Create a Group
Click "Create a Group" and enter your trip name (e.g. "Goa 2026"). You'll get a unique 6-digit code instantly.
→
2
👥
📢 Add Your Friends
Share the code with your travel buddies. They click "Join with Code" and enter it to access the same group from their phone.
→
3
💸
📝 Log Expenses
Add each person to the People tab, then log every expense in the Expenses tab — split equally or set custom amounts per person.
→
4
✅
🧮 Settle Up
Hit "Calculate Settlement" in the Settlement tab. TripWise shows exactly who pays whom with the fewest transactions. Export to PDF for your records.
